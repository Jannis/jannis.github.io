## Autodidact Musician I have taught myself how to play various instruments over the years and tried my luck in a number of small underground bands, with music styles ranging from Black Metal to Stoner and Post/Indie Rock. Having moved around a fair amount recently, my current focus is the solo project *Ufobuddha*. ### ![Ufobuddha](/images/ufobuddha-logo.png) Ufobuddha is energetic, loud, noisy and spacious rock that occasionally drops into a state of melancholia. I set no rules for what the music should sound like, so most songs exist purely by virtue of jamming and experimenting. A first album is planned for 2014.
