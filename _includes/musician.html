## Autodidact Musician I have played different instruments over the years and have tried my luck in a number of small underground bands, with music styles ranging from Black Metal to Stoner and Post/Indie Rock. Having moved around a fair amount recently, my current focus is the solo project *Ufobuddha*. ### ![Ufobuddha](/images/ufobuddha-logo.png) Ufobuddha is energetic, loud, noisy and spacious rock that occasionally drops into a state of melancholia. It builds on simple roots and repetetive, layered patterns. There are no set rules or limits as to what the result should sound like and most songs are the results of extensive jams and experimentation. A first album is planned for 2014.
